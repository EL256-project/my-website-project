Writers & Journalism Club

The Writers' Club provides a platform for aspiring journalists, poets, and authors. Students learn the art of storytelling, critical analysis, and news reporting, preparing them for the world of media and communications.

Key Skills: Creative Writing, Editorial Skills, Public Speaking, and News Anchoring.
