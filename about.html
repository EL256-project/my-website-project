About Us

Our History

Lubiri Secondary School was founded by Sir Edward Mutesa II in 1956. Originally located inside the King’s palace, it has grown into an academic giant in Kampala.

Governance Structure

We maintain a strong, transparent leadership led by the Board of Governors and the PTA Committee, ensuring student success remains our top priority.

Major Activities

Academics: A powerhouse school sending thousands to University.

Sports: National champions in Handball, Basketball, and Football.

Clubs: Home to the vibrant Writers' Club and "The Lubirian" magazine.
